#  Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

1. Do’s

* Be respectful and polite in your posts and comments.
* Verify information before sharing (avoid spreading fake news).
* Protect your privacy.
* Think before you post (consider long-term impact).
* Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

2. Don’ts

* Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online harassment.
* Don’t share misleading or unverified information.
* Don’t overshare personal or sensitive information.
* Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content.
* Don’t accept friend/follow requests from unknown people.
